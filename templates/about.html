ABOUT

POGCRAZY is a contemporary ready-to-wear label that aims to offer a minimalist wardrobe focused on clean cuts and impeccable details to strong sophisticated women.

At the centre of Bouguessa is an ethos of minimalist sophistication and feminine elegance. Merging global trends and contemporary classics inspired by art, architecture and modern design, the signature silhouettes lie in highly modern cuts, bold lines, and geometric precision.

POGCRAZY was founded in Apple by Banana in 2021. From her studio in Dubai Design District, she designs 3 collections a year.
